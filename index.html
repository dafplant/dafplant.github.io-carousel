Hello, my name is Chris

An aspiring web developer

I'm about...

Music and Technology

I daydream about finding new ways of sourcing, creating and listening to music. I can’t be introduced to enough pioneering music or technology and I want to be a part of the movement. I’ve been instrumental in the founding of a start-up telecommunications company. I’ve worked there for 8 years. I build computers, navigate software and solve IT problems on a daily basis. I love being challenged by technology. Now it’s time to marry my passions and improve my skills so that I can operate on another level. I’ve found real enjoyment in coding. A deeper knowledge could unlock my potential to develop something new and contribute to a world of technological possibilities.

Making

I make music. I started drumming when I was 10, studied for a Music Diploma at Academy of Contemporary Music and now play with various bands, performing in cover acts and creating new sounds of various genres. I make other stuff, too! I made a dining room table this year, using reclaimed oak at a friend’s workshop. I’d like to make a drum kit next!

Travel

I travel. The destinations I choose are generally of geological interest. My favourite place to go is California where I am surrounded by pioneering tech startup company HQs, celebrity musicians, sunshine and incredible scenery and nature. I love hiking and have recently taken up rock climbing.

Environment

I care about our planet. One of the many things I love about technology is that it offers ways of developing sustainable solutions to the world’s problems. I have a keen interest in wildlife and a fascination for geology and astronomy. I aspire to work in an environment that is not detrimental to the global climate.

Why Founders and Coders

Founders and Coders is an ideal platform for me to realise my lifelong ambition of becoming a Coder and Website Developer. I am fascinated in technology. I recognise the need for world leading coding techniques in a fast growing industry and am excited to be a part of it’s growth. I want to fully focus on learning more skills so that I can contribute to the development of website functionality in new ways. Since engaging in Founders and Coders, I have been enthused by it’s teaching and learning ethos. I am looking forward to expanding my own knowledge and collaborating with other students to accelerate our learning.
